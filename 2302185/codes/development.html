Summary of *Twelve Pillars* (Chapters 4-7)

Chapter 4: Achieve Your Goals

This chapter emphasizes the importance of setting clear goals. Goals provide direction and purpose, shaping who you become as you pursue them. Achieving goals isn't just about material success but personal growth.

Application to My Life:

I have set specific academic and career goals, such as excelling in advanced levels of mathematics and securing internships in the tech field. The process of achieving these goals will help me build discipline, problem-solving skills, and resilience.

Chapter 5: The Proper Use of Time

Time is your most valuable resource. This chapter stresses the need to manage it wisely by prioritizing tasks that align with your goals and avoiding distractions.

Application to My Life:

I plan my days carefully, ensuring that I allocate enough time to both academic work and personal development. By using time management tools, I stay focused on my studies and avoid time-wasting activities that donâ€™t contribute to my goals.

Chapter 6: Surround Yourself With the Best People

The people you surround yourself with influence your success. This chapter encourages finding a supportive and motivated community that pushes you to grow and stay focused.

Application to My Life:

I seek out study groups and professional communities where people are driven and focused on similar goals. By surrounding myself with motivated peers, I stay inspired to continue working toward my goals.

Chapter 7: Be a Life-Long Learner

This chapter highlights the importance of continuous self-education, beyond formal schooling. Lifelong learning helps you stay relevant and adaptable in a fast-changing world.

Application to My Life:

Outside of my coursework, I regularly read books and articles about new technology trends, advanced mathematics, and self-development. This continuous learning keeps me sharp and better prepared for my future career in computer science.
